This list of resources was originally compiled by Student Shop Treasurer Evan Dorn in 2001. The information was significantly updated in 2006, but there are undoubtedly still errrors and outdated information. We hope you find this to be helpful in whatever projects you do. If you have sources you would like to tell others about, or corrections to make regarding the below information, email us--we would appreciate your input.


Hardware
Home Depot
Address: 1625 S Mountain Ave, Monrovia, CA
Phone: 626.256.0580
Hours:

Everything you'd find at a typical home improvement store. Primarily convenient because it's 24 hours. Get on the 210 and head East. Get off at Myrtle and go straight. You'll pass Juniors Tools on the Right, Home Depot will be there in a block or two.
Orchard Supply Hardare
Address: 3425 East Colorado Boulevard, Pasadena, CA 91107
Phone: 626.395.7115
Address: 452 Fair Oaks Avenue, South Pasadena, CA 91030
Phone: 626.403.8115
Hours: Mon.-Fri. 8-9; Sat. 8-8; Sun. 8-7

Orchard is mostly like a slightly smaller, slightly more expensive home depot without any real lumber. It has a garden section and nursery. I go to the one on Colorado because it's on the way to Home Depot, so I can get on the freeway and keep going if OSH doesn't have what I want.

Berg Hardware
Address: 495 North Altadena Drive, Pasadena, CA 91107
Phone: 626.793.6161
Hours: Mon.-Fri. 8:30-5:30; Sat. 8:30-1:30

A great place with all sorts of funky components you won't find elsewhere, like rings and pulleys, an extensive selection of threaded rod, etc.
A-Zal Fastener & Supply Co.
Address: 4905 Telegraph Road, Los Angeles, CA 90022
Phone: 323.262.0259; 800.327.8289;
Hours:

This is where the Caltech stockroom buys boxes of screws, bolts, etc. You can go straight there to get better prices, and to find items you can't find anywhere else.
True Value
Address: 409 North Fair Oaks Avenue, Pasadena, CA 91103
Phone: 626.792.2196
Hours: Mon.-Fri. 7-7; Sat. 8-6; Sun. 8-5

Hardware only, has a lot of paint and decorating, and lighting stuff. A better selection of pipes and bins of fasteners than Home Depot or OSH. Go West on california or colorado, the right on Fair Oaks. It's on your left just past the freeway.
Crown City Hardware
Address: 1047 North Allen Avenue, Pasadena, CA 91104
Phone: 626.794.1188
Hours: Mon.-Sat. 8:30-5:30

A nice old-fashioned funky hardware store in Pasadena. They have a website: www.restoration.com. Excellent selection of fancy and old hardware like doorknobs and lightswitch plates, etc. Many useful bits of hardware loose in bins, the old-fashioned way.

Lumber
Caltech Carpenter Shop
Address: 1047 North Allen Avenue, Pasadena, CA 91104
Phone:
Hours: Mon.-Fri. 9-4:30

At the north end of the building with central stockroom in it. Standard construction-grade softwood materials. Also carry high-quality hardwoods. You're better off getting the low-grade stuff at Home Depot, etc.
Home Depot

See above.
Davis Lumber Co.
Address: 1787 East Walnut Street, Pasadena, CA 91106
Phone: 626.792.7104
Hours: 9-4:30

Construction-grade pine, fir, and redwood. Go north to Walnut and turn right, or go east to Allen and turn left. Corner of Allen and Walnut..
Arroyo Hardwood & Mouldings
Address: 2707 East Foothill Blvd., Pasadena, CA 91107
Phone: 626.304.0021
Hours: Mon.-Fri. 8:30-5:30; Sat. 9-4

Beautiful selection of hardwoods and decorative softwoods. Expensive, though --it seems to start around $10 per boardfoot for thin board and go up. They also have veneer in rolls.

Metal
Caltech central warehouse
Address: Right next to the student shop.
Phone:
Hours: Mon.-Fri. 9-4

2024 and 6061 aluminum, cold rolled steel, and stainless steel in many shapes and sizes. Various bits and pieces of copper, bronze, and assorted plastics. Prices are a bit high.
Industrial Metal Supply Co.
Address: 8300 San Fernando Rd., Sun Valley, CA 91352
Phone: 323.849.3184
Hours: Mon.-Fri. 7-5; Sat. 8-2

They sell all kinds of metal: Cu, Fe, Sn, Pb, Al, etc., and various combinations thereof, in various forms: sheet, bar, plate, hex, etc. They have a huge warehouse, and discounts for large purchases (~$100?) Their prices are decent. They also have welding supplies.
Queen City Steel
Address: 2801 North San Fernando Boulevard, Burbank, CA 91504
Phone: 818.846.3446
Hours:

South a few blocks from Industrial Metal Supply in Burbank. Sells lots of steel beams, sheets, bar, square stock, rectangular stock, etc. Good selection of ornamental cast-iron. Also some tools.
Metal Depot Inc.
Address: 14334 Firestone Boulevard, La Mirada, CA 90638
Phone: 714.521.5494
Hours:

There's a place South on the 5 that calls itself Metal Depot, and looks like it sells cool scrap metal, and I've always wanted to go there.
Industrial Pipe & Steel Co.
Address: 9936 Rush Street, South El Monte, CA 91733
Phone: 626.443.9467
Hours: Mon.-Fri. 7:30-5; Sat. 7:30-1

Metals of all shapes and sizes. Also a good source of industrial supplies like lubricants, sanding discs, etc. Down in the south duarte industrial park near a number of other great stores.

Electronics
Radio Shack
Address: 1715 E. Colorado Blvd.
Phone: 626.449.4527
Hours: Mon.-Fri. 9-8; Sat. 9-6; Sun. 10-6

Typical radio shack. I only go here if Duvac is closed.
C&H Surplus Sales
Address: 2176 E. Colorado Blvd., Pasadena, CA
Phone: 626.796.2628
Hours: Mon.-Fri. 8:30-5:30; Sat. 9-5

Warehouse of everything surplus. Motors, switches, capacitors, pumps, oscilloscopes, lenses, glassware, everything. Great place to browse, definitely the best place to find motors. Very knowledgeable and nice salespeople who will help you find what you need and explain stuff you don't understand. Go east on Colorado, it's on the right a ways past PCC.
Duvac Electronics
Address: 1759 E. Colorado Blvd., Pasadena, CA 91106
Phone: 626.796.3291
Hours: Mon.-Fri. 8:30-6:00; Sat. 9-5:30

Lots of great stuff at reasonable prices - this what radio shack should be. If you're building circuits, it's the place to go. It closes early, though.
Frys Electronics
Address: 2311 North Hollywood Way, Burbank, CA 91505
Phone: 818.526.8100
Hours: Mon.-Fri. 8-9; Sat. 9-9; Sun. 9-7

A vast warehouse of everything electronic. Really, really big. However, it's gone much towards computer and consumer electronics lately, so Duvac is actually often better if you're trying to build circuits from scratch. Go west on the 210/134. Take the 5 northtowards sacramento. Get off at Hollywood Blvd and turn left. It's on your right in a couple miles, after the Burbank airport.

Tools/Industrial Supplies
Industrial Pipe and Steel Co.

See above. Many tools, including welding. It's the only local place for some of the heavier-duty industrial stuff, both tools and supplies (cutting oil, large sanding discs, etc. See the metals section for info.
Home Depot

See above. Reasonable source for hand tools. See the hardware section for info.
Harbor Freight Tools
Address: 3660 East Foothill Boulevard, Pasadena, CA 91107
Phone: 626.304.9272
Hours: Mon.-Fri. 8-6; Sat. 8:30-5:30; Sun. 10-4

Tools only. Most of their hand tools and such are cheap and you get what you pay for. But they have some big stuff you don't see at home depot (engine lifts, welders), a good selection of pneumatic tools and equipment, and it's a good place to buy safety gear and bulk consumables. Take the 210 E. To Rosemead, get off at the "rosemead north" exit. It's on your left at the intersection with foothill, U-turn at foothill to get into the parking lot.
Sears
Address: 3801 E. Foothill bl.
Phone: 626.351-3400
Hours: 10-9

A pretty good hardware section. Home of Craftsman hand tools which are nice because of the lifetime warranty. Follow the driving instructions for Harbor Freight but turn right on Foothill and get in the left lane.
Juniors Tools
Address: 123 West Pomona Avenue, Monrovia, CA 91016
Phone: 626.303-8063
Hours:

Tools aimed at the small contractor, so they have somewhat bigger stuff than Home Depot or Sears. You can find (some) real machines there, though not the biggest ones. Kind of in-between Home Depot and Grizzly. Follow the driving instructions to Home Depot.
So Cal Airgas
Address: 525 Commercial Street, Glendale, CA 91203
Phone: (818) 240-5917
Hours: Mon.-Fri. 9-5 (guess); Sat. 9-1;

A good place for welding equipment and supplies..

Mail Order
MSC Industrial Supply
Address: www.mscdirect.com
Phone:

There's an MSC big book catalog in the shop library. A good source for pretty much everything. They have a warehouse in LA, so stuff gets here in 2 days even via UPS ground most of the time.
Grizzly Industrial
Address: www.grizzly.com
Phone:

A reasonable source for tools that isn't as expensive as Delta, MSC, or the like. Mostly just tools, few supplies. Sometimes they aren't the cheapest, though, so comparison shop.
